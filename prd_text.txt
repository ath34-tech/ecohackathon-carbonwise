Sustainability Intelligence Platform March 9, 2026

Product Overview
CarbonWise AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform designed to help users compare the lifecycle carbon emissions of different vehicle types including Electric Vehicles (EV), Hybrid vehicles, and Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles.
The platform analyzes driving patterns, regional energy sources, and lifecycle emissions to recommend the most environmentally sustainable vehicle for each user.
Beyond consumer recommendations, the platform aggregates anonymized mobility data to provide valuable insights for governments, businesses, and sustainability researchers through an analytics dashboard.

Problem Statement
Vehicle sustainability comparisons are often misleading because they focus only on fuel effi- ciency. In reality, emissions occur across the entire lifecycle of a vehicle including:
Manufacturing emissions
Battery production emissions
Fuel or electricity usage
End-of-life disposal
Consumers lack access to tools that provide accurate lifecycle comparisons. Governments and mobility companies also lack aggregated data about sustainable mobility decisions.

AI Agent System
The platform uses a multi-agent system to analyze sustainability data.

User Query Agent
Parses user input and extracts structured driving data.

Driving Pattern Agent
Calculates total lifetime driving distance and energy consumption.

Lifecycle Emission Agent
Computes lifecycle emissions including manufacturing, usage, and disposal phases.

Energy Source Agent
Determines electricity carbon intensity based on geographic location.

Recommendation Agent
Generates the final sustainability recommendation.

System Architecture
